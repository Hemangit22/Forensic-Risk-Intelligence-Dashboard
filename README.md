# Forensic Risk Intelligence: Optimizing Audit Performance and Capital Protection
🛡️ **End-to-End Analytics Project: Fraud Detection & Resource Optimization**
**Scale:** 1,000,000+ Forensic Audit Records | Full-Stack Analytics Case Study

---

## 📖 Project Overview & Business Case
As enterprises scale AI-driven financial processes, traditional "Post-Audit" frameworks fail to keep pace with modern Risk Velocity. This project investigates the intersection of AI adoption and forensic risk, identifying a critical "Primary Attack Window" where 68% of incidents occur within the first 10 days—a pace that manual audits cannot match.

**The Mission:** Transition from reactive manual auditing to a preemptive, automated "Velocity-Gating" strategy to protect **$1.20M** in at-risk capital.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
Derived from core forensic audit requirements, this project was built to solve four specific challenges:
1.  **Quantify Risk Velocity:** Identify the precise temporal window (Time-to-Risk) where manual intervention fails.
2.  **Diagnose Operational Efficiency:** Map firm-wide performance to identify "Manual Bottlenecks" where high workloads correlate with risk exposure.
3.  **Optimize Resource Allocation:** Use a Risk-Return Frontier to determine which audit processes should be automated, maintained, or sunsetted.
4.  **Maximize Capital Protection:** Project the financial ROI of shifting to automated "Velocity-Gating" controls.

---

## 🛠️ The Technical Workflow (Big Data Engineering)

### 📊 1. ETL & Data Architecture (Alteryx)
* **Multi-Source Ingestion:** Engineered a robust pipeline to ingest and normalize 1M+ rows of fragmented audit logs (Kaggle) and specialized fraud suites (Feedzai).
* **Cleansing & Transformation:** Performed complex multi-join operations and null-value remediation to resolve data inconsistencies across global records.
* **Feature Engineering:** Generated temporal "Maturity" flags and standardized currency headers for high-fidelity modeling.

### 💾 2. Behavioral Pattern Extraction (SQL)
* **Temporal Analysis:** Developed advanced Window Functions and CTEs to calculate "Time-to-Risk" (TTR).
* **Risk Segmentation:** Created complex conditional logic to categorize incidents by "Velocity" and "Impact."
* **Aggregation:** Summarized behavioral attack patterns across "Big 4" firms to identify operational outliers.

### 📈 3. Financial Logic & ROI Validation (Excel)
* **Risk-Return Frontier:** Built a quantitative model to simulate the financial impact of shifting from manual labor-intensive audits to automated "Velocity-Gating."
* **Sensitivity Analysis:** Validated a **$1,192,800** capital protection opportunity by calculating the reduction in manual overhead.
* **Operational Benchmarking:** Modeled the "Employee Workload vs. Risk Exposure" correlation to pinpoint failure thresholds.

### 🎨 4. Executive Data Storytelling (Tableau)
* **Narrative Design:** Translated 1M+ data points into a 4-Chapter Interactive Story for C-suite stakeholders.
* **Visual Intelligence:** Developed a Strategic Quadrant Analysis (Scatter Plot) to identify "Manual Bottlenecks."
* **Dynamic UX:** Implemented "High-Impact Highlighting" and drill-down tooltips for firm-level investigations.

---

## 🔍 Key Strategic Findings
* **The 1.0-Day Gap:** Analysis revealed an average 1.0-day "Time-to-Risk," proving manual audits are too slow.
* **The 10-Day Threshold:** Evidence shows "Velocity-Gating" at the 10-day mark is the optimal point for automated intervention.
* **The Efficiency Paradox:** High employee workloads (e.g., Deloitte) directly correlate with higher risk exposure.
